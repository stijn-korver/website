About

Built to solve
hard problems

Korver AI Solutions is an independent AI consultancy. We design and build custom AI systems for organisations where generic tools aren't good enough.

Mission

AI that actually
fits the problem

Most organisations that want to use AI face the same frustration: the tools that exist weren't built for their specific situation. They're either too generic, too expensive, or too difficult to integrate into real workflows.

Korver AI Solutions exists to close that gap. We work with mid-size organisations, research institutions, and government bodies to build AI solutions that are purpose-built, fully integrated, and explainable to every stakeholder who needs to trust them.

We don't sell licences. We don't offer subscriptions to platforms. We build things that solve specific problems — and we hand them over completely.

How We Operate

Four principles
we don't compromise on

These aren't values on a poster. They're constraints that shape how we work on every project.

01
Understand before building

We don't open a code editor until we understand the problem, the data, and the people who will use the output. Skipping this step is how projects fail.

02
Build clean

Simple, maintainable solutions over clever ones. The measure of good engineering is whether the next person — or the client — can understand and extend it.

03
Make it explainable

AI that stakeholders can't understand doesn't get used. Every system we build is designed to be explained — from how it works to why it made a particular decision.

04
Deliver full ownership

When a project ends, the client owns everything — code, models, documentation. No lock-in, no dependency on us to keep the lights on.

Why Independent?

Senior expertise
without the overhead

Large consultancies bring large teams, long timelines, and fees structured around both. For organisations that need a specific, well-defined AI system built properly, that model often doesn't make sense.

Korver AI Solutions operates lean by design. Every project is handled with full technical accountability — no delegation to junior staff, no work lost in handoffs between departments. You work directly with the person who builds the system.

That's not a limitation. For the problems we take on, it's an advantage.
